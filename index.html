About Department


The Department of Computer Software Engineering is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge required to design, develop, and maintain reliable and efficient software systems. Our program blends strong theoretical foundations with hands-on practical experience in programming, software design, algorithms, databases, web development, mobile applications, and emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence and cloud computing. Our mission is to produce software engineers who are innovative, technically skilled, and ready to contribute to solving real-world problems through technology. With a focus on teamwork, critical thinking, and continuous learning, we prepare students for successful careers in software development, research, and entrepreneurship. We are committed to academic excellence, industry collaboration, and fostering a supportive learning environment that inspires creativity and innovation.

Our Services


The CSE department offers a range of academic and technical services aimed at supporting students, staff, and the wider community. These include:

1. Academic Programs - Diploma, undergraduate, and certificate programs in Software Engineering and related fields.

2.Project Supervision & Guidance - Support for final year and personal software projects, including mentoring and code review.

3. Workshops & Training - Regular training on programming, web development, mobile apps, databases, cybersecurity, and more.

4. Software Development & Consultancy - Designing and developing custom software solutions for schools, businesses, and NGOs.

5. Career & Skill Development CV reviews, interview preparation, and soft skills training tailored for tech careers.
